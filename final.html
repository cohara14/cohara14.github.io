About Us

Montana Raft Outlet is a Montana based company that works to ensure that all your water needs are met. Whether you are fishing, floating the scenic Smith River, drinking your way down the river. We've got you covered. Montana Raft Outlet specializes in offering high quality raft rentals, as well as selling you your own vessle to take to the waters. We offer premium raft accessories and guided raft trips through the heart of the Montana water ways.

Montana Raft Outlet has happily served Montana for years offering nothing but the best quality rafts, service, and memories that a company can offer. Come check out Montana's beautiful waters today.

MADE IN AMERICA. We offer rafts that are all made in America, as well as the highest quality raft frames, straps, oars, and oarlocks that the market has to offer. Our primary goal is making sure you have the best equipment you can find for the best prices you can find. Come check out our instore or online stock today.
